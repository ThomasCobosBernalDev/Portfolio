About

U.S. Marine with nearly three years of experience leading teams, managing operations, and solving complex problems in high-pressure environments. Roles as an Infantry Team Leader, Scout, and Certified Combat Marksmanship Coach have developed my leadership, analytical thinking, and technical training abilities, all directly transferable to engineering and technology fields. Born in Ecuador and raised in Houston, Texas, I bring a unique perspective and adaptability shaped by diverse life experiences and global teamwork. Now transitioning toward AI engineering, I’m building a strong foundation in coding, web development, and Python, with certifications in Responsive Web Design and ongoing study in data analysis, algorithms, and machine learning fundamentals. My goal is to combine my operational leadership and disciplined problem-solving mindset with advanced technical skills to develop innovative, data-driven solutions.
